Introducing AWS UG Portugal

Learn, Discover, Inspire

Our AWS community gladly welcomes you

Mission Our mission is to create a vibrant and supportive community for AWS enthusiasts in Portugal. We aim to foster knowledge sharing, collaboration, and professional growth among AWS users, helping individuals and organizations leverage Amazon Web Services to its fullest potential. Vision Our vision is to establish AWS User Group Portugal as a leading community that not only inspires and educates its members but also drives technological innovation and excellence in the region.
We are proud to have a supportive community of nearly 1500 members who share their knowledge and experiences. Still recovering from the Pandemic, we have been nurturing and creating more activities for our members. We are grateful for the support of our distinguished sponsors, who help make our events possible. If you're interested in partnering with us for one of our future sessions, we'd be happy to hear from you and work together to create a meaningful and inspiring event.
What We Do At AWS User Group Portugal, we organize a variety of events and activities designed to enhance the AWS knowledge and skills of our members: Monthly Meetups: Regular sessions featuring expert talks, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities. Want to do Workshops & Training: Practical, in-depth training sessions on specific AWS services and solutions. Hackathons & Challenges: Engaging events where members can apply their skills to solve real-world problems. Webinars & Online Sessions: Virtual events to ensure continuous learning and engagement, regardless of location.

Here's how we can help you grow: Knowledge Sharing: Learn from AWS experts, industry leaders, and peers through our interactive sessions and collaborative forums. Leadership Opportunities: Get involved in organizing events, leading projects, mentoring new members, and building your leadership and management skills. Networking: Connect with fellow AWS enthusiasts, professionals, and organizations, opening doors to new opportunities and collaborations. Project Support: Turn your ideas into successful projects with access to a network of skilled individuals, resources, and potential partners. Career Development: Gain hands-on experience, earn AWS certifications, and explore new career pathways in the ever-evolving field of cloud computing.
